Project Overview

The Dr. Manasa Endocrine Center website features 40 SEO-optimized and responsive pages, including profiles for two doctors, over 25 healthcare services, blogs, health packages, and a chatbot for seamless appointment booking. It integrates a CMS for easy content updates and management.

Key Features

  • 📄 Dedicated pages for two doctors with detailed profiles
  • 🌟 Center of Excellence showcasing 25+ healthcare services
  • 📝 SEO-optimized service pages for endocrinology and general surgery
  • 📚 Blogs and articles managed via CMS
  • 💼 Careers page with job application management
  • 📦 Bookable health packages for patients
  • 💬 Chatbot for easy appointment booking
  • 🏥 Facilities page detailing diagnostics, therapy, and pharmacy
  • 📅 Landing pages for promotions and events
  • 📹 News and media with embedded YouTube videos

Technical Details

  • 💻 Frontend: Next.js with Server-Side Rendering
  • 🎨 Styling: Tailwind CSS for fast and responsive design
  • 📂 CMS Integration: Manage pages, blogs, and multimedia content
  • 📧 Email notifications for form submissions
  • ⚡ Optimized for fast loading and cross-device compatibility
  • 📊 SEO-friendly metadata and structured schema
